Basic Algebra


Algebra.

Algebra is a part of mathematics that swaps out certain numbers in equations for letters.

This is usually the case when the swapped number is unknown as of yet.

Example:

10 + a = 15



Example Explanation:

In the example 10 + a = 15, so if you take 10 away from both sides of the equation

your left with a = 5.


Most Algebraic questions will ask you to find the value of the letter, (a in our case), just

as shown above.


Try it yourself Question:

a + 5 = 10, find the value of 'a'
