Highest Common Factor


Highest Common Factor.

The Highest Common Factor of 2 numbers there largest shared factor.


Example:

the factors of 30 are (3, 10, 2, 5) and 50 are (5, 10, 2, 5) now find the largest shared factor (10).


To find 2 numbers Highest Common Factor, first find each of their Factors then the largest Factor

that both numbers share is the Highest Common Factor.
