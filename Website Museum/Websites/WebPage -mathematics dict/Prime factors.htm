Prime Factors.

The Prime Factor of a number is any of its factors that are prime.


Example:

The Prime Numbers of 60 are 2, 2, 3 and 5


To find a numbers Prime Factors use whats called a Prime Factor Tree, it looks like the diagram above:


First you write 2 numbers that multiply together to make the original to the sides, keep doing this

untill you can only go further by using 1, (you never use 1 in a Prime Factor Tree).
